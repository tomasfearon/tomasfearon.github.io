Travel

The nearest airport is Trondheim Værnes.

There are direct coach and train connections to Trondheim city centre from the airport. Both will take roughly 40 minutes. The coach generally is more frequent and convenient.

Please don't hesitate to ask us if you need some assistance in planning your travel.


Accommodation

We recommend you stay within Trondheim city centre ("Trondheim Sentrum" or "Midtbyen" in Norwegian). Trondheim is very walkable, and transport links in the city are good.

There are many good AirBnB options in the city centre.

Things to see and do in Trondheim

Nidaros Cathedral


Nidaros Cathedral has existed in some form since 1070 AD, and is the most popular attraction in Trondheim. You'll get a great look inside during the ceremony, but you should consider taking some more time for a more detailed viewing.

Trøndelag Folk Museum


Sverresborg, Trøndelag Folk Museum is one of the country's largest cultural history museums with collections of buildings (including the stave church from Haltdalen) and objects, archive material and an extensive historical photo archive.

Nidelva River


The Nidelva river both frames the city centre of Trondheim and offers good fishing opportunities, for those so-inclined. You'll also be able to see landmarks such as Gamle Bybro - the old town bridge, the 17th century working-class neighbourhood of Bakklandet, and the world's first-and-only bicycle lift!

Many more ideas for things to do, and more tourist information can be found on trondheim.no
